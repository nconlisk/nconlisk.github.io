Background:

Working at the interface between engineering and medicine, I employ problem solving and technical engineering skills to answer clinically relevant questions in the fields of cardiovascular biomechanics and orthopaedic engineering.

I hold a PhD in Orthopaedic Engineering from the University of Edinburgh UK, and a BEng (honours) degree in Mechanical Engineering (specialising in biomedical engineering) from Galway-Mayo Institute of Technology Ireland.

Specialties include:

1) Finite element mesh generation from medical imaging data (CT/MRI).
2) Patient specific modelling.
3) Orthopaedic implant design.
4) Design and manufacture of in vitro test apparatus.
5) Musculoskeletal and Cardiovascular biomechanics.
6) Scripting, and high performance computing.
7) 3D model creation and visualisation in CAD.
8) Advanced manufacturing processes (manual/CNC machining/3D printing).
